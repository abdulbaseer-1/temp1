Legs Workout

This leg workout combines compound exercises for the quads, glutes, and hamstrings, while not overlooking smaller muscles and ensuring training in all three planes. It is designed to build size, strength, and explosiveness.

1. Warm-Up and Squat

Start with squats, the foundation of any leg workout. Do a few reps with sub-working weight to warm up your joints, then perform a "touch-up" set with 10% more weight to prepare for your working sets.

  • Work from sets of 5 to 25 reps.

2. Posterior Chain Compound Exercise

Pick one based on your goal:

  • Barbell Hip Thrust – For strength.
  • Glute Ham Raise (GHR) – For hamstring engagement.

3. Hi/Low Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squat

This unilateral exercise targets dynamic stability and works the frontal plane. Vary your torso position to hit both quads and glutes.

4. TKE Drop Lunge

Focuses on terminal knee extension. Achieve full lockout on each rep for maximum stimulation. Use a resistance band to increase difficulty.

5. Goblet Adductor Lunge

This move targets the adductor muscles. Squeeze your legs together to return to a standing position, engaging the inner thighs.

6. Hip Band Ladder

Strengthens the abductors with lateral steps. Keep toes pointed forward and work up to 10 reps in each direction.
