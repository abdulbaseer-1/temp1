Biceps & Triceps Workout

Home arm workouts often don’t produce the same results as those workouts performed for your biceps and triceps in the gym. That doesn’t have to be the case, however. In this video, I’m going to give you a complete home workout for arms that is going to help you build bigger biceps and triceps without feeling like you are leaving gains on the table. The key to the effectiveness of this perfect arm workout is the use of some common household items that you may never have known were so valuable for creating killer arm exercises.

This home arm workout consists of both a beginner and an advanced version so everyone has something to do. The variations on the two versions, however, are minimal as most of the exercises can be done by all with a small modification made to the amount of weight being lifted. That said, the differences will be highlighted and the complete workouts will be posted for each level at the end of the video as always.

As mentioned, this arm workout is designed to help you build bigger arms when you are not training in a gym. There are no sacrifices made here, so you can feel free to continue to do this even if training at the gym is an option for you.

Most of the arm exercises included here are ones that are designed to maximize hypertrophy and muscle growth. The rep ranges performed are going to largely be in the lower to mid rep range with the goal being to modify the exercises in such a way to create muscle failure within those ranges.

Here is how to construct the perfect home arm workout:

HOME BICEPS EXERCISES

  • WEIGHTED CHINUPS
    3-4 Sets x 6-8 reps (choose a weight that helps you to reach failure within this range)
    Beginners may swap in seated chinups here if chins are too difficult.
  • BICEPS CHINUPS
    2-3 sets of 10-12 reps to failure
    Beginners may swap in inverted chin curls if this exercise is too difficult.
  • WAITER’S CURLS
    2-3 sets of 10-12 reps to failure
  • LIP BUSTER CURLS INTO NEGATIVE CHIN HANGS
    2-3 sets of 15-20 reps on the lip busters immediately into a negative chin to failure

HOME TRICEPS EXERCISES

  • WEIGHTED UPRIGHT DIPS
    3-4 Sets x 6-8 reps (choose a weight that helps you to reach failure within this range)
    Beginners may swap in feet assisted dips here if dips are too difficult.
  • TRICEPS PUSHDOWNS
    2-3 sets of 10-12 reps to failure
  • TRICEPS PUSHAWAYS
    2-3 sets of 10-12 reps to failure
  • TRICEP PUSHUP TRIO
    2-3 sets to failure on each pushup as one giant drop
    Beginners will perform each of these variations from their knees.

If you are doing this workout just to try it out, be sure to give it your best effort. The key to home workout effectiveness is to put in as much effort as you would in the gym. Many get comfortable training in their home and approach their workouts with a different level of intensity. As long as you train hard here, the arm exercises chosen for this home arm workout are going to deliver bigger biceps and triceps in a relatively short period of time.
